Ready Camp Privacy Policy

About This Policy

This policy applies to any information collected by the Ready Camp iOS application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") which was created and is maintained solely by John Quagliata.

Information Collected

I do not use, save, or have access to any data collected by the application.

No personal information is collected.

Updates and additions to the camping list and the names of campers entered during a packing session is not personal data and is only stored on your device. The information is never transferred to any servers or services.

Information Usage

The information entered into the application is strictly used to support the functions and features of the application and is never transmitted outside of your device.

Sharing

No information entered into the application is available outside of your device and thus is never shared with any third parties.

Accessing, Changing, or Deleting Information

Information collected by the application is only stored on your device. Management of the data is available through the user interface within the application.

Camping list items can be accessed, changed, and deleted using the controls within the application. Camper names for a packing session are deleted when the packing session is ended.

Removing the application will remove all of the data.

Data Retention Policy

No personal data is collected by the application. No other data entered into application is saved or retained for any amount of time outside of your device.

Your Consent

By using the application you consent to this privacy policy.

Contacting Me

For questions regarding this policy or any other inquires about the application please email readycamp@quagsoft.com.
